These are all of the liner notes to On Juhu Beach (and their translations) not
included in the main On Juhu Beach page, other than the basic biographical
information written in English. Pieces of paper are transcribed in
counterclockwise order to their position on the insert, starting at the top
left, since this gives the songs in order. Each scrap of paper is transcribed
then translated before moving on to the next. Words written in parentheses are
implied but not actually written in Japanese (except for the possible plural
of friend(s) in Burned My Tongue, where it's not possible to tell which it is
due to the limited context and lack of singular vs. plural forms in Japanese).
